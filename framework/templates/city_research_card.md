# City Research Card

Use one card per city or region. It keeps each place's facts together so you can compare places fairly later. Fill it in from trusted sources, and record each source in your Source Log.

```text
City or region: __________________________________________
Why people go there: _____________________________________
One memorable fact: ______________________________________
Top sights (star the ones you can't wait to see):
   - ____________________________
   - ____________________________
   - ____________________________
Food and culture notes: __________________________________
Travel time from a likely nearby city: ___________________
How many days might we want here? ________________________
Possible downsides (crowded? far? costly?): ______________
Best season fit (does it match our Checkpoint 1 season?): ____
Sources (also in my Source Log): _________________________
Date checked: ____
Planning assumption (something I'm assuming a grown-up should check): ______
Needs adult verification? (yes / no) ____
Final decision status: (researching / shortlist / skip / save for future) ____
```

Remember: each card is a movable block. If a grown-up later changes which city you fly into or out of, you move a block -- you do not start over.
