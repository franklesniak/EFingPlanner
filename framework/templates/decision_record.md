# Decision Record

Use one Decision Record for each big decision. Together, these records make your **decision log** -- the story of what your family decided and why. Each of the six checkpoint decisions gets a record here too, so nothing important is decided and then forgotten.

```text
Decision (what are we deciding?): ________________________
Date: ____
Options we considered:
   - ____________________________
   - ____________________________
   - ____________________________
My recommendation: _______________________________________
My reasons (the important part): _________________________
Evidence / sources (from my Source Log): _________________
Trade-offs (what we gain, what we give up): ______________
Does a grown-up need to decide or verify anything? _______
Final family decision: ___________________________________
```

Tip: your first decision record is your season recommendation at Checkpoint 1. Keep them in order so you can see how the plan grew.
