# Current Family Travel Assumptions

This page holds the rough starting points the grown-ups set at setup. Everything here can change -- it is a starting point, not a final decision. A grown-up owns and updates this page; the child reads it so their plan stays realistic.

Keep no sensitive personal data here (no exact booked dates, hotel names, confirmation numbers, or payment details).

```text
Rough season window (or a few candidate months): ______________
   ("this can change")
Rough budget band -- a not-to-exceed signal, not a final number: ______________
   In kid-sized form the child works with (circle or fill one):
     - about $____ per person per day, or
     - about $____ per day for the family, or
     - "we can afford this hotel tier: ______"
   (The full trip total is a grown-up number, kept off the child's worksheet.)
Rough trip shape (a grown-up, flight-shaped call; "this can change"):
     - Round trip (in and out of one city), or open-jaw (in one city, out of another)? ____
     - Likely arrival city: ____________
     - Likely departure city: ____________  (leave open if not known yet)
Known constraints:
     - Mobility: ____  (none known is fine)
     - Dietary: ____   (none known is fine)
     - Sensory: ____   (none known is fine)
     - Medical: ____   (none known is fine; keep specifics with the adults, off the page)
AI helper -- a grown-up call (default no): yes / no ____
   (If yes: the child completes Session 09 before using any AI tool, and the grown-ups
    follow the adult-operated pattern in the privacy and safety rules.)
```

If your family has never been to the destination, naming just the arrival city is a fine answer. Leave the shape and exit city open and firm them up later on the full Core path (by the City Shortlist checkpoint). For the First Taste, leaving them open is fine.
